This is my family! We are a close knit group since we are the only family we have here in Washington. There's my mom (who's name is also Ana), my dad Juan (who goes my Johnny), and my giant little brother Juan Pablo.

Color Guard...

This is an activity that is near and dear to my heart. Color guard involved the art of dancing while also spinning either a rifle, flag, or sabre. I've been doing this activity since I was a freshman in high school, but sadly had to quit since there are no groups to join out in Spokane.

Luckily I am able to go back to my old school during the summer and help teach the art of color guard. Hopefully if I keep practicing I might be able to join a drum corp next year before I age out.
